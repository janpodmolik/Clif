A screen time app your pet depends on.

Uuumi is a virtual pet that lives on a floating island and reacts to everything you do on your phone. The more you scroll, the harder the wind blows. Less scrolling means calmer winds and a happier companion.

HOW IT WORKS

Choose which apps bring the wind — social media, games, anything that pulls you in. Pick a wind preset each morning: Gentle, Balanced, or Intense. Then go about your day. Your pet feels every minute.

BLOCK DISTRACTIONS

When the wind gets too strong, Uuumi blocks your distracting apps with a shield. Lock your apps, take a break, and let the wind calm down. If the wind hits 100% and you still want to keep scrolling — you'll have to blow your pet away to continue. The choice is yours.

EVOLVE YOUR PET

Protect your pet each day and earn essences. Use them to unlock new evolution forms. Every evolution is a milestone you built — and every new form is proof your habits are changing.

TRACK YOUR PROGRESS

See where your time goes with daily and weekly screen time reports. Track your streaks, watch your trends, and see the full history of every pet you've raised.

EARN & GROW

Stay under your limits and earn coins. Take committed breaks for bonus rewards. Spend coins on new essences and unlock new evolution paths for your next companion.

Give your screen time a face. Ready to meet your Uuumi?
