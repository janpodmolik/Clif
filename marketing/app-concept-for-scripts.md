# Uuumi — How the App Works (for marketing scripts)

## Core Concept

Uuumi is a screen time management app with a virtual pet. You pick specific apps you want to limit (Instagram, TikTok, etc.), set a daily time limit, and your pet reacts to how well you respect that limit.

**It's NOT about staying off your phone entirely.** It's about respecting the daily limits YOU set for the apps YOU choose.

## The Pet

- Lives on a small floating island (a grass-covered cliff)
- Starts as a simple blob
- After one day, you choose an **Essence** (Plant, Moss, Shroom, Troll, etc.) which determines the pet's evolution path
- Pet evolves through phases (up to 6–8 stages) — each day you respect your limit, your pet evolves further
- Pet has moods: **happy** (low usage), **neutral** (medium usage), **scared** (near the limit)

## Wind System (core mechanic)

Wind is a visual metaphor for how much you've used your blocked apps today:

| Wind Level | Usage     | Pet Mood | What Happens                        |
|------------|-----------|----------|-------------------------------------|
| None       | 0–4%      | Happy    | Calm, pet is relaxed                |
| Low        | 5–49%     | Happy    | Light breeze, pet wiggles slightly  |
| Medium     | 50–79%    | Neutral  | Moderate wind, pet is stressed      |
| High       | 80–100%   | Scared   | Strong gust, pet struggles to hold on |

- **Wind rises** when you use blocked apps
- **Wind falls** when you take a break (app actively blocks your selected apps during breaks)
- If wind reaches **100% and you keep going** → pet is **blown away**

## Blow Away (the stakes)

When your pet is blown away:
- **All evolution progress is permanently lost**
- Pet is archived (gone forever)
- You start over with a new pet from scratch

This is the emotional hook — you genuinely don't want to lose your pet.

## Breaks

- You can take **intentional breaks** during the day
- During a break, selected apps are **actively blocked** (shield screen appears)
- Wind decreases during breaks
- Breaks are the tool that helps you stay within your daily limit

## Daily Presets (difficulty levels)

Users pick a preset that defines how strict their daily limit is:

| Preset   | Time Before Blow Away |
|----------|-----------------------|
| Gentle   | 20 minutes            |
| Balanced | 12 minutes            |
| Intense  | 8 minutes             |

Each morning, you confirm or change your preset for the day.

## Evolution

- Pet evolves **once per day** if you respected your limit the previous day
- Evolution is NOT gradual ("the more you do X") — it's binary: either you kept your limit or you didn't
- Each essence has a unique visual evolution path (6–8 phases)
- Fully evolved pet = weeks of consistent limit-respecting

## What Uuumi is NOT

- It's NOT about total phone screen time — only the specific apps you choose
- It does NOT punish you with notifications or guilt — the pet's reaction IS the feedback
- The pet does NOT "grow when you stay off your phone" — it evolves when you respect your daily app limits
- It's NOT a timer or a simple blocker — the wind system + pet creates emotional investment

## Correct Marketing Language

**YES:**
- "Your pet evolves when you respect your daily limits"
- "If you exceed your limit, your pet gets blown away — permanently"
- "You choose which apps to limit and for how long"
- "Take breaks to keep the wind down and your pet safe"
- "The wind picks up every time you open a blocked app"

**NO:**
- ~~"Grows when you stay off your phone"~~ (it's about specific apps, not the phone)
- ~~"The more you respect limits, the more it evolves"~~ (it's daily, not proportional)
- ~~"It blocks your phone"~~ (it blocks specific apps you choose)
- ~~"A tiny creature that grows"~~ (it evolves through distinct phases, not gradual growth)
