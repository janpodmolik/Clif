# Uuumi — Video Scripts

Short-form scripts for TikTok / Reels / Shorts. App is LIVE on the App Store.

**Format:** chunk-based beats (no second timestamps). Each chunk = one spoken sentence + one visual cut.

**Mechanic accuracy:** before editing or writing new scripts, check [app-concept-for-scripts.md](app-concept-for-scripts.md) — wind is a metaphor for % of daily limit (mood, not physical position), pet evolves daily binary, app limits specific apps you choose (not the whole phone), blow away = permanent loss.

**Angle:** Build-in-public daily check-in. Day 2 = first evolution payoff.

## Origin & vulnerability

### Script 1 — "My wife asked me to build this"
**Hook template:** Origin story
**Angle:** Personal origin — how Uuumi started.

1. "My wife kept asking me if I could help her spend less time on her phone."
2. "So I built her an app. It's called Uuumi."
3. "It's not like other screen time blockers. It's built around a tiny pet that lives on a floating island."
4. "If you respect your daily limits, your pet evolves. If you don't — it gets blown away. Permanently."
5. "She loves it. If you struggle with screen time too, download Uuumi. Link in bio."

### Script 2 — "I used to be the worst one"
**Hook template:** "Never thought…" / vulnerability
**Angle:** Admitting your own addiction.

1. "I used to spend six hours a day on my phone. And I'm not proud of that."
2. "I tried every screen time app out there. They all felt like punishment."
3. "So I thought, what if instead of punishing yourself, you had a reason to care?"
4. "That's why I built Uuumi. You get a pet that evolves every day you respect your limits. But if you go over — it's gone forever."
5. "It sounds weird, but it actually works. It's free to download. Link in bio."

### Script 3 — "The moment I knew I had a problem"
**Hook template:** Specific moment / emotional hook
**Angle:** Realization moment.

1. "I was sitting at dinner with my wife, and I realized we were both just scrolling."
2. "Not talking. Not even eating. Just… scrolling."
3. "That moment hit me hard. I'm a developer, so I thought, I can fix this."

### Script 6 — "I almost gave up on this app"
