## Hook bank

Hooky seřazené podle priority. V [scripts.md](scripts.md) je každý existující scénář napojený na konkrétní hook template.

### Priority

1. **Day X series** — "Day 2 of fixing my screen time…" (HLAVNÍ ZBRAŇ, payoff = evoluce peta)
2. **Person A vs Person B** — "Person A uses Screen Time. Person B has a friend that dies if they scroll too much."
3. **Never…** — "Never thought a virtual pet would fix my doomscrolling. But here we are."
4. **There are way too many apps…** — "…that just show you a number. This one made me actually care."
5. **Don't lose a friend** — koncept ok, exekuce flopla, potřebuje konkrétní kontext
6. **This is why you should always…** — "…have something to lose when you scroll."
7. **I see so many people using…** — "…screen time blockers that they ignore in 2 seconds."
8. **Day in a life** — "A day in my life, but my screen time decides if my pet survives."
9. **What's the difference?** — "What's the difference between me last month and me now? I had something to protect."
10. **If your X looks like this** — "If your screen time looks like this, you need a friend on the other side of that scroll."

### Backlog (k vyzkoušení)

Generické viral templates — vyžadují konkrétní Uuumi payoff, jinak skončí jako "DON'T LOSE" flop.

11. **It took me X years…** — "It took me 4 months of building to learn this, but I'll show you in 60 seconds why every screen time app fails."
12. **Give me 60 seconds…** — "Give me 60 seconds to show you why a virtual pet fixed my screen time when 5 other apps couldn't."
13. **Most people don't realize…** — "Most people don't realize Apple Screen Time has a button that erases the entire point of it. It's called 'Ignore Limit.'"
14. **Never X again…** — "You'll never tap 'Ignore Limit' again after losing a pet you spent 14 days evolving."
15. **I'm finally revealing how I…** — "I'm finally revealing how I cut my screen time from 3 hours to 30 minutes — and it wasn't willpower."
16. **This is the brutal reason…** — risk: zní jako guru content. Funguje jen s vulnerability follow-up.

## Pravidla pro tvorbu

### DO
- **Pattern interrupts každé 2–3 sekundy** (cut, zoom, text overlay)
- **Talking head je OK** — autentický raw obsah outperforms polished
- **Original audio + voiceover**
- **B-roll appky v relevantním momentu** (ne někde jinde)
- **Captions** — 60–70% lidí kouká bez zvuku
- **Hook v prvních 3 sekundách**
- **Loop / cliffhanger na konci**

### DON'T
- "Hey guys, today I want to talk about…"
- Statický záběr bez střihů
- Hook bez kontextu ("DON'T LOSE")
- Příliš polished produkce
- "Blokuje ti telefon" — Uuumi limituje **konkrétní vybrané appky**
- "Pet roste když jsi off phone" — pet evolvuje **denně, binárně**, když dodržíš limit
- "Vítr ho fyzicky odfukuje" — vítr je metafora pro % limitu, **mění mood**, ne pozici
