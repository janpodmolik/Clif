# I almost gave up on this app

**Hook template:** Vulnerability + underdog (the format that hit 1.8k on YouTube Shorts) + personal mission + beta CTA
**Format:** Talking head. Single take preferred. Quiet, honest energy.
**Length:** ~25s
**Goal:** Convert vulnerability + mission into early TestFlight signups + community feedback loop.

---

## Script

1. "Two months ago, I almost deleted everything."
2. "The code was broken. The animations were glitching."
3. "But then I opened the app, and my little pet was just sitting there on its island. Waiting."
4. "I know it sounds silly. But I couldn't quit on it."
5. "Because I want this thing to actually help people spend less time on their phones."
6. "It's called Uuumi. Still in testing — TestFlight link in my bio. Try it, then tell me what works."

---

## Delivery notes

- **Tone:** quiet, honest. Not dramatic, not salesy. The format that hit 1.8k was hands-in-face vulnerability — borrow that energy.
- **Pacing:** chunks 1–4 are the personal story (slower, contemplative). Chunk 5 is the mission shift. Chunk 6 is matter-of-fact CTA — don't speed up here, hold the calm.
- **Eye contact:** look at camera the whole time. No glances away. The trust comes from steadiness.
- **Captions:** mandatory. 60–70% watch on mute, and this script lives or dies on the words.
- **B-roll (optional):** chunk 3 could cut to actual app screen showing pet on island, calm. ≤2s. Otherwise stay on talking head.

## Description

"Two months ago I almost deleted my own app. Here's why I didn't. Uuumi — TestFlight link in bio."

## Risks to watch

- **Don't oversell chunk 5.** "Help people spend less time on their phones" must sound like a quiet conviction, not a TED talk. If it sounds rehearsed, the vulnerability of chunks 1–4 is wasted.
- **TestFlight link must work** before posting — broken link kills trust on a vulnerability post specifically (audience already invested emotionally).
- **"Tell me what works"** is a real ask. Be ready to actually reply to DMs that come in. If you ghost the people who message, this format burns.

## Variations to try later

- **15s ultra-short:** drop chunks 2 and 5, go from "almost deleted everything" → "pet sitting on its island" → "couldn't quit" → CTA. Loses mission framing, gains completion rate.
- **Reorder for hook:** open with chunk 5 ("I want this to help people spend less time on their phones"), then "Two months ago, I almost gave up." Different curiosity gap — mission first, struggle second.
- **Add a specific number:** chunk 5 could become "…spend less time on their phones — even one hour less a day matters." Concrete > abstract.
